## What we're building

Support is part of the product. A slow widget during onboarding is a lost customer. A pricing surprise is a broken trust. So we built the support tool we wished we had. One that stays fast, stays simple, and stays out of your way.

- **A widget that loads in under 100ms.** Your customer clicks chat, and it's already there.
- **An inbox your team learns in a day.** No onboarding course, no certification, no training budget.
- **AI that drafts from your knowledge base.** Free to suggest, honest about pricing, only billed when it actually closes the ticket.
- **Deep integrations.** Linear, Slack, GitHub, and more. Support lives where you already work, not in another tab.
- **Native apps, built natively.** iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Android. Each one designed for its platform, not ported to it.
- **An API for the rest.** Build your own integrations on top of SupportWire. The [docs are right here](https://developers.supportwire.app).

## How we build

- **One impactful feature, every month.** No bloat, no feature-chasing.
- **Speed is a feature.** We don't trade performance for checkboxes.
- **Pricing stays predictable.** No surprise line items, no meters that punish you for growing.
- **Support stays human.** A real person, not a chatbot wall.

We build in the open. See [where we're headed](https://supportwire.featureos.app) before you commit.
